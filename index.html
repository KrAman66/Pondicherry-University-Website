Student Academic Management System (PU-SAMS)

|

The software SAMS is an online-platform that is meant to manage entire gambit of CBCS – It covers Student details, registration of courses, approval of registration by the faculty, allocation of courses to the faculty, conduct of classes, online fee payments, student attendance, internal assessment, end-semester assessment, conversion of marks to grades, declaration of results, grade card generation & printing, No-dues clearance, verification of Course Completion, Consolidated Mark statement, Provisional & Degree Certificate and all reports.. The software works on the principle of differential access and need-to-know basis.

Important Instruction !!!

Any queries / feedback email related to PUSAMS / PURAMS / etc. may be sent to sams@pondiuni.ac.in and not to sams@pondiuni.edu.in
