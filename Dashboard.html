PU Campus Connect WiFi - Instructions

The process of connecting to CAMPUS CONNECT Wi-Fi is given below:
  1. Enable Wi-Fi in your Laptop/Smart phone/any other hand held device, etc.
  2. Connect your device to Campus Connect.
  3. Enter the Registered mobile number and Voucher PIN received from SAMS.
  4. OTP will be generated and sent to your registered Mobile.
  5. Enter the 4 digit OTP number in the next page.
  6. After submission, password will be sent to your registered Mobile number.
    • a. For Students - the userid will be your Register Number.
    • b. For faculty/Officers/Staff - the userid will be your Employee Code
  7. Enter Userid and Password and start surf.
For any problems during the above steps from 3 to 6, kindly contact
